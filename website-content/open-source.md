# Open Source

## Open Source From Day One

Valygate is completely open source.

GitHub: `https://github.com/cloaky233/Valygate`

## Why We Made That Choice

We are building developer infrastructure.

That means trust matters, and openness matters.

People should be able to inspect how the gateway works, understand how requests are translated, see how secrets are handled, and follow the product as it evolves.

## What Open Source Means Here

- visible architecture
- transparent product direction
- community feedback before and after launch
- issue-driven development
- a path for contributions and collaboration

## Our Belief

The best infrastructure tools are the ones people can read, trust, and improve.

We want Valygate to be one of those tools.

## Community Signal

We are not pretending to know everything on day one.

We want developers to tell us what is missing, what is confusing, and what should be built next.

## Copy Lines

- Open source, because trust should be inspectable.
- Built in the open, with feedback as a core input.
- The product should belong to the developers who use it.
- Let the community help shape the control layer for AI providers.
