# FAQ

## What is Valymux?

Valymux is an LLM gateway, proxy, and observability platform for developers who need one stable way to work across multiple AI providers.

## Who is it for?

AI engineers, backend teams, startups, and anyone tired of rebuilding provider-specific glue code every time the ecosystem changes.

## What problem does it solve?

It solves the integration chaos that comes from juggling multiple providers, different request formats, changing model capabilities, and scattered observability.

## Is the product ready today?

Not yet.

We are still building the core backend and product foundation, and the site is here to show progress, invite feedback, and start building an audience early.

## What makes Valymux different?

It focuses on three things at once:

- safety
- speed and concurrency
- observability

And it does that without throwing away developer experience.

## What providers are supported first?

OpenAI and Anthropic are the first provider paths in the codebase, with a design that can expand beyond them.

## Is it open source?

Yes.

GitHub: `https://github.com/cloaky233/Valymux`

## Will the core be free?

We want to keep as much of the value as possible free, especially the parts developers need most.

## What is the MVP timeline?

We are aiming for the end of April 2026.

## Can I give feedback now?

Yes.

That is exactly what we want.

We are building this for developers, and the best feedback comes early.

## What should I expect next?

More product progress, more clarity around the roadmap, and more proof that Valymux is becoming a real platform rather than just an idea.
